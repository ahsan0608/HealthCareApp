# HealthCareApp
This Health app connects you with your own healthcare providers lots of other features like - 
** consult with doctor
** book appointments
** medicine reminder
** search blood bank
** nearest hospital
** nearest pharmacy
** health blog
** symptom checker 
** make payments
** access health data/ history.
